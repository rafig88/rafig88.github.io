About Project

This site is about basic bicycle information along with basic security for your bike. The reason this site is about bicycles is due to my love for bike riding. It all started when I bought a cheap bike and use it as a means of tranportation to go Brooklyn College. Eventually I started to bike ride to different destinations on my off days from college. In the summer I would go to different spots with my friends and enjoyed the scenary. We would often go to the beach since its cheaper than going by public transportation. Overall, I hoped you enjoy the information that is provided to you and hit me up if you got any questions. Thank You!!!

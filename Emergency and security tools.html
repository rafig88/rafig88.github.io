Tools

Before you start your bike travels it is highly recommended that you bring certain tools with at all times. A multi-tool kit is very important to bring in case you need to do an emergency repair on your bike. Another thing you should bring is bike tubes. Reason why it is important is due to the fact that you don’t want to be stranded in the middle of the road. I provided you with a Youtube link that will show more on what to bring on your bike journey.



Now that you reach your destination and you want to take a break to explore the site by foot. How do you secure your bicycle? How do you prevent your bicycle from getting stolen? What I recommend is to get a bike lock. A bike lock can drastically improve your chances of your bike being stolen. The most prefer bike lock to get is get is the u-lock. Reason being is due to it's sturdiness. It will take bicycle thieves a while to fully cut a u-lock. This decreases the apprehension of constantly thinking if your bicycle will get stolen.
